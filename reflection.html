‚úàÔ∏è Aerospace Engineering using Entrepreneurial Skills:

My career of choice, aerospace engineering, often requires many of the entrepreneurial skills that we've learned in this course:

  • Leadership - Many aerospace engineers work in small groups on specific tasks on a project. Leadership is essential in managing these small teams to get the most out of every individual. Leadership in this context includes giving all team members the correct roles, managing everyone's work to ensure deadlines are met, bringing all the parts together to create a final product, and overall managing the team to get work done efficiently.
  • Organization - Organization is vital in aerospace engineering since often in aerospace engineering we are working on many tasks at once with multiple different components that if not organized well can become overwhelming and messy. Organization means using a calendar to organize your time, having organization in the literal sense with all your designs and parts correctly categorized along with working with your team by organizing the overall project to seamlessly flow together.
  • Analytical - Analytical skills are vital in aerospace engineering as they provide engineers with the ability to analyse the many problems that occur with aircraft. Analytical skills are used when designing airframes to ensure they meet the specified criteria, analysing the different components of the aircraft and the different data to make the most reasonable decisions, and to correctly bring all the data together for effective execution of our plans.
  • Project Management - As I‚Äôve mentioned before, aerospace engineers often work in small teams on very specific parts of the overall project. Project management is vital to ensuring that the project flows smoothly with the team. Managing a project includes making sure that the task is clearly defined to members, having a clear and concise plan to accomplish the set goal, and tracking progress to ensure the plan is being executed.
  • Technological Skills - Apart from the practical technical skills needed to develop aircraft, it is important for engineers to be able to use and adapt to different softwares for all sorts of tasks such as design, testing, and team collaboration. It is also vital that engineers are able to use presentation softwares and online tools to represent their work to senior engineers. Without these technical skills and familiarity with online software engineers may not be able to convey effectively their ideas and work.
